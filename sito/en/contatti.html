Casa e Bottega is just outside the narrow lanes of the historic centre, on one of Manfredonia's main commercial streets. During the day you have everything within easy reach: groceries, pharmacy, cafés, all kinds of shops. In the afternoon and evening, when the shutters come down, the street turns quiet and peaceful, perfect for resting after a day at the sea or exploring the Gargano. Parking is no problem: you can leave your car comfortably nearby, just a few steps from the entrance.
