La Dimora

Intimate, silent, you feel at home

High ceilings, king-size bed with quality linens, smart TV with Netflix, desk corner for those who can't fully disconnect. Everything is essential, nothing is superfluous. It's the room where you feel at home, perhaps because it truly is.

Crafted in detail, artisanal, awaited hospitality
